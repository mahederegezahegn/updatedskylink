Exhibitors

The technology event aims to provide a platform for various public and private organizations in the technology sector to showcase their products and services.
The event expects participation from established tech companies, startups, software developers, telecom providers, electronics manufacturers, e-commerce platforms, education/training providers, consulting firms, research centers, government agencies, non-profits, and hardware manufacturers.
These organizations cover areas such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, augmented/virtual reality, Internet of Things, enterprise software, mobile apps, cloud services, cybersecurity, electronic components, e-commerce, ICT skills, digital literacy, tech consulting, R&D, ICT policymaking, bridging the digital divide, and manufacturing computer hardware.
The event provides an opportunity for these organizations to promote their latest innovations and solutions.

Why Exhibit at Our Event?

Showcase Your Products and Services

As an exhibitor, you'll have a dedicated space to showcase your products, services, and innovations. This allows attendees to interact with your offerings firsthand and gain a better understanding of what you provide.

Direct Engagement with Potential Customers

Events attract attendees who are interested in the industry or field that the event covers. Being an exhibitor provides you with the opportunity to engage directly with a targeted audience who are more likely to be interested in your offerings.

Lead Generation

Exhibiting at the event allows you to collect leads from attendees who express interest in your products or services. These leads can be valuable for future marketing efforts and potential sales.

VISA PROCESS TO ETHIOPIA

Visas are required for all foreign visitors to Ethiopia, with the exception of nationals of Kenya, Djibouti, and Diplomatic and UN passport holders.

You must first register online and receive your registration confirmation before applying for a visa.

Once registered, please email a copy of your passport to the Secretariat at info@ictexpoethiopia.com.

The Secretariat will send you the necessary documents to apply for your conference e-visa over the following schedules:

  • First batch of approvals: April 21
  • Second batch of approvals: April 28
  • Third and last batch of approvals: May 5
Once on the website, you will select:
  • Conference Visa
  • Government (as the inviting institution)
  • Ministry of Innovation and Technology
You will need:
  • Electronic copy of your passport
  • Electronic copy of your passport photo
  • Electronic copy of the application letter (will be sent to you)
  • Valid Credit Card to pay $32 online
  • Additional documents for visa extension

The process is straightforward, and you will secure your visa approval within 36 hours or less.

If you are not able to process your visa online, please notify the Secretariat for further support.

CUSTOMS

If you are shipping materials to showcase at the expo, please note that you will have to follow the customs law of the country mainly:

  • You cannot sell any exhibit materials
  • Exhibit materials are subject to duty taxes
  • You are responsible for shipping and clearing your materials directly
  • The Secretariat will assist in getting your chosen clearing agent with the necessary documents to clear your goods
  • Please note that you will need to send a detailed packing list and commercial invoice on your letterhead showing each item with quantity and value
  • As certain electronic materials may require additional processes, you are strongly recommended to ship your products for the exhibit at least 20 working days ahead
  • You will need a delivery address in Ethiopia for your goods, and note that the Secretariat cannot play that role for your goods
