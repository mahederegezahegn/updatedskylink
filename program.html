ICT Expo Ethiopia 2020 constitutes part of a series of events taking place from May 14 until May 18, all of which make up what is termed as the “Technology Week”. The other events taking place during that week include StartupEthiopia, STI Award, and a Conference.

ICT EXPO ETHIOPIA

ICT EXPO ETHIOPIA is Ethiopia’s largest and most extensive ICT Exhibition, Panel discussions, and Summit.
ICT Expo Ethiopia is where established technology service vendors, private and public sector decision-makers, integrators, and users of ICT technology for business or public good as well as disruptive startups with unique solutions come together to create important networks and build valuable partnerships. From enterprise solutions to large telecom operators, ICT Expo Ethiopia brings the latest technology innovations to benefit organizations operating or planning to engage in the expanding Ethiopian market. The Expo serves as a catalyst for collaboration and cooperation between foreign and local technology companies and a multitude of partners in the ICT ecosystems. The event combines unrivaled conference content with a major exhibition and a well-crafted networking opportunity. Besides the panels, the Expo comprises of Business to Business meetings, Business to Customer meetings, Government to Government meetings, and Government to Business meetings. As Africa’s diplomatic capital and a rising conference tourism destination enchanting Addis Ababa provide the appropriate setting for business travelers and those curious to explore the opportunities of the wider Ethiopian market. Startup Ethiopia

  • Identify and showcase dynamic startups from all over Ethiopia
  • Position Ethiopia as an emerging startup ecosystem
  • Create a platform for startups to connect to investors
  • Raise venture capital and impact investor interest in Ethiopian startups
StartupEthiopia Organization and Elements

StartupEthiopia aims to become a nationally and globally recognized annual event, growing its participation in terms of highly qualified startups and investors starting with 100 semi-finalist startups, 10 finalists, and 25 investors and a total attendance of 1000 in 2020.

StartupEthiopia is organized through a public-private partnership between the Government of Ethiopia, represented by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology, and blueMoon, a private incubator, and seed investment company. Moreover, a host of partners would be engaged, including various Ministries and agencies, regional bureaus, universities, industry associations, peer incubators and accelerators, media and event partners, corporate sponsors, youth groups, and civic organizations, among others. In addition, a group of 100 volunteer youth ambassadors would be engaged for the running of the 2-day event.

StartupEthiopia 2020 will be held as an event of Ethiopia Tech Week, alongside ICT-Expo, in the Millennium Hall, on May 16-17.
